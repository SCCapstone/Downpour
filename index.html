Introducing Pweek

The Pohnpeian Language-Learning App

Introducing Pweek, the ultimate language learning app that makes learning Pohnpeian easy, fun, and engaging! Pweek is designed to help you learn Pohnpeian at your own pace, with lessons and quizzes that reinforce your learning. With Pweek, you'll learn not just the language, but also the culture and history of the Pohnpeian people. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, Pweek has something for everyone.

Why Learn Pohnpeian with Pweek?

Learning Pohnpeian with Pweek is a unique and exciting opportunity to explore the rich culture and history nestled in the island of Pohnpei in Micronesia. Pweek was developed as a language-learning Android app, similar to Duolingo, but with a focus on underserved language groups. Our app is specifically designed to teach users the language of Pohnpeian, spoken only by an estimated 45,000 native speakers across the world.

By using Pweek, users can learn to read and speak the language through lessons plan, quizzes, and cultural insights. The app offers a practical and efficient way for users to gain a good working knowledge of the most commonly used phrases, which can help them to navigate the island and communicate with the locals. With Pweek, learning Pohnpeian is not just about the language but also about how we connect with the culture and history of the people who speak it.

Features of Pweek

The Ultimate Language-Learning App

Lessons

Our app provides engaging and effective lessons that cover reading, writing, speaking, and understanding Pohnpeian. Lessons feature interactive exercises and audio recordings.

Quizzes

Test your Pohnpeian language skills with engaging quizzes! Each quiz features multiple-choice questions that track your score, allowing you to measure your progress and improve your language proficiency.

Search

Find what you need quickly with the search page in our app. Explore the culture and geography of Pohnpei by searching for specific information on different parts of the island.

Explore

Our explore feature unlocks a world of hidden gems in Pohnpei. Simply tap on any image to reveal a wealth of information and insights.

Bio

Users can create a profile to track lesson and quiz progress, and customize their bio for other users and Pohnpeians to learn about them.
